The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

OkenoChile had been staring out of his window for about an hour now. On his desk, there were copies of The Master Key System, The Alchemist, Think and Grow Rich , The Intelligent Investor, Self Reliance, The Art of War , Steve Jobs: A biography, Elon Musk Tesla, SpaceX, and the Quest for a Fantastic Future, Shoe Dog, Creativity Inc, Self Reliance by Ralph Waldo Emerson, The Subtle Art of Not Giving a Fuck, and all the other books he had read, including a thumbed copy of A Brief History of Mankind. These had been only a few of OkenoChile's selected readings since college, and the longer he spent time behind the walls of his room reading, writing, and coding, and learning to produce music, the more he felt he needed to be free.

He had spent the last ten years mostly closed inside his room, sitting on a computer doing writing, academic research, programming, reading, and, recently, learning to produce music. This created a thought inside his head: That, come rain come sunshine, he was an individual committed to making his own decisions in life and that he was destined to do great things. Although OkenoChile faced many challenges from distracting petty behaviours of feeble-minded neighbours, continuous trolling and harrassment by fools, belittling political propaganda directed towards him by political buffoons and ethnic political puppets, he vowed to be an Arch of his own life. That no authority or individual will take away his freedom to decide on his personal life matters and that nobody will decide, dictate nor influence his personal life choices in way that is contrary to his wishes. This, he considered to be his own responsibility. Towards achieving his greater vision, OkenoChile chose focus and hardwork while ignoring the sideshows of jealous nitwits who could be perfectly characterized as idle-minded, illiterate, bootlicking, barking, snitching, and persecuting pettyfoggers.

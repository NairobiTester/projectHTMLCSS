Procedure for cooking pork

  1. Chop the 1Kg pork meat into pieces
  2. Boil in salted water for 15-20 minutes until tender
  3. Remove water if there still is and heat the pork until it is dry
  4. Put 50g butter in a pan and heat then pour the pork inside
  5. Mix for 5 minutes then pour chopped onions and garlic and crashed ginger
  6. Blend tomatoes and pour them in then add salt and pepper
  7. Cook for two minutes then remove

In the late 19th century, electricity was a relatively new and exciting technology. Inventors and scientists around the world were working to harness the power of electricity and put it to practical use. One such inventor was a young man named Thomas. With this new invention, he was able to produce a steady stream of electricity that could be used to power all kinds of devices and machines. Word of Thomas's invention quickly spread, and he soon became famous as one of the world's leading experts in the field of electricity.
